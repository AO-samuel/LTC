Online Classes

LTC holds online classes for the various areas of programming that is been taught every weekday to keep the mentees busy, they are always given assignments to get their hands dirty with code during the weekends.

Bootcamps

LTC organizes bootamps for those who are interested in getting more knowledge or those who want to pick up programming as a point of study.With these bootcamps students/newbie's get to meet their mentors live and get to interact with them.

Hackathons

After every bootcamp LTC organizes a Hackathon to test the students, that is how LTC knows it's exceptional students and these exceptional students get awesome prizes after which they are offered a Job opportunity for the community.
